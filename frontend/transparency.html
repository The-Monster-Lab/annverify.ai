EDITORIAL INDEPENDENCE DECLARATION
✓ANN Verify operates as an editorially independent fact-checking platform. No investor, funder, advertiser, or external stakeholder has any influence over the verdicts, scores, or editorial conclusions published on this platform.
✓Editorial decisions — which claims to check, how to score them, what verdict to assign — are made solely by ANN Verify's editorial team based on evidence and our published methodology.
✓ANN Verify does not accept requests from any individual or organization to fact-check specific claims on their behalf.
✓ANN Verify's editorial output is not controlled by the state, a political party, or a politician. We do not receive funding from political parties or government bodies for our editorial operations.

Nonpartisanship Policy

ANN Verify does not advocate for any political position, party, ideology, or policy outcome. Our fact-checking applies the same standard to every claim regardless of who makes it.

⚖️
Equal Treatment
Claims from politicians, media outlets, or institutions of any political alignment are evaluated using identical criteria.
🔬
Evidence-Only Verdicts
Verdicts are determined exclusively by evidence quality, source credibility, and logical consistency. Political preference is not a factor.
🌐
Geographic Balance
Fact-checks cover global topics — not concentrated in any single country or regional perspective.
📣
No Advocacy
ANN Verify does not take policy positions on the issues it fact-checks. We report on factual accuracy — not whether a policy is good or bad.

ANN Token & Editorial Independence

AI News Network is developing the ANN Token as a utility mechanism for platform participation and user incentives. The following declaration ensures no ambiguity about its relationship to editorial operations.

ANN Token Declaration
UTILITY TOKEN
The ANN Token is a utility token designed to incentivize platform participation, reward contributors, and power community governance features within the ANN ecosystem.
What the ANN Token is NOT:
  • ANN Token does not represent equity, ownership, or a financial claim on AI News Network Pte. Ltd.
  • ANN Token holders do not have any influence over fact-check verdicts, editorial decisions, or content moderation.
  • Token revenue does not create any dependency or obligation toward token holders in ANN Verify's editorial operations.
  • Token launch timing and mechanisms are commercial decisions made separately from editorial governance.
